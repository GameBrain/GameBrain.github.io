Building Overview

The Dark Elixir Drill is a resource collector building. It will produce dark elixir every hour that can be used to upgrade troops, heroes and more. The dark elixir that’s produced will be stored in a Dark Elixir Storage building.

You can use gems on the Dark Elixir Drill to speed it up and produce twice as much dark elixir per hour.
