Overview

Clash of Clans is a popular game created by Supercell. Clash of Clans is a strategy game where players build their own village, unlock powerful warriors and attack villages created by other players. The game is free to play but it does offer in-app purchases to speed up the game play.

Troops

Clash of Clans focuses on attacking other players with troops to gain resources and steal trophies. Resources are used to upgrades your buildings and troops to make them more powerful. Trophies are used to match you with opponents to attack.

Spells

You’ll use Spells to help your troops during a raid. There are many different types of spells but they all help your troops in some way or another. Spells do not get created as fast as your troops so make sure you use them wisely.

Buildings

You’ll be using many different types of buildings to create your village in Clash of Clans. Some buildings are used to defend your village from attacks while others are used to help you attack other players. Visit our buildings page for more information about the different structures you can create in the game.

Traps

You can improve your village’s defense by adding traps to it. They’re hidden from an attacker and can be used to improve weak areas of your base. You’ll need to rearm your traps after they’ve been used. You can do so by clicking on your Town Hall and selecting the option to rearm all traps.
