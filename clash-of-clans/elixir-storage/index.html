Building Overview

The Elixir Storage is a building that’s used to hold your elixir resources in a single location. When a user collects elixir from their Elixir Collector it will be transferred to one of their Elixir Storage buildings.

Your resources must be in a storage building before you can spend them on something. Make sure to keep the Elixir Storage upgraded so that you don’t become full on resources and are unable to transfer them from the collector to the storage.

It’s easier to protect your resources when they’re in a storage building rather than a collector building. Storage buildings have more hitpoints than collector buildings. There’s also fewer storage buildings which makes them easier to defend.
