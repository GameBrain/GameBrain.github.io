Building Overview

Walls are the main protection for your village. Use them to create base layouts that make it hard for enemy players to attack.

It’s best to avoid creating a double layer of walls. Wall Breakers deal splash damage to walls so they will be able to easily penetrate the defense if you use a layered wall strategy. We recommend using the walls to create lots of small compartments in your base.

Although the Wall is expensive to upgrade, it’s definitely worth it. You’ll be a prime target for higher level players if you choose to focus on upgrading your Town Hall without upgrading the rest of your defense.

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the Wall are shown below. The model changes drastically at each level. The lower level Walls are made by wood, stone, steel and eventually gold. The models in the middle use pink and purple crystals. The higher level Walls switch back to steel and have spikes added to them.
