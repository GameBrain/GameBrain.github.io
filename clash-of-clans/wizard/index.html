Troop Overview

The Wizard is an extremely powerful ranged damage dealer that does splash damage. It has the highest DPS in the game per housing space but it also has very low hitpoints. The Wizard is best paired with a tanking unit such as the Giant or the Golem.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Housing space: 4

Training time: 8 minutes

Movement speed: 16
