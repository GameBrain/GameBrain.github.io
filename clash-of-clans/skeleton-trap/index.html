Trap Overview

The Skeleton Trap will release several skeletons to attack your enemy when it’s activated. The trap is similar to your Clan Castle because the skeletons that it releases will follow enemy troops around the map to attack them.

The Skeleton Trap can be configured to attack ground or air units. Toggle the trap to ground mode to attack ground troops or toggle it to air mode to attack air troops.

Trap Information

Trigger radius: 5 tiles

Damage type: Single target

Favorite target: Any

Targets: Ground or air
