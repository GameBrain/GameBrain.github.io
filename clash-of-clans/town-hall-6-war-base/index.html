General Information

On this page of our guide, we will show you a war base layout for Town Hall 6 in Clash of Clans. The layout is optimized for clan wars and minimizing the amount of damage your opponent can do to your base.

We’ve created a list below that shows you the priority for protecting each resource you have. The list ranks each of them from most important to least important. This guide follows the priority shown below.

Protection Priority

The protection priority for a Town Hall 6 war base is:

  1. Trophies

Trophies and resources are not actually lost during a clan war. The purpose of a war base is to reduce the amount of damage an attacking player is able to do; the layout is built as if it needs to protect trophies.

TH6 War Base Layout

TH6 War Base Summary

Town Hall – This should be placed in the center of your base. If the Town Hall is destroyed in battle it gives the attacker one star. The more stars your attacker gets the more trophies you will lose.

Clan Castle – This should be placed near the center of your base and is a key structure for defense. Always have units in the castle so that it can be used for offense and defense.

Wizard Tower – These towers do amazing splash damage. Like mortars, they are great against units that are close to one another. Youâ€™ll gain an additional tower at this level for a total of two. The two wizard towers should be placed on the perimeter of your base on opposite sides of each other. The towers will play a key role in defending your gold storage and your mortars.

Archer Tower – Archer towers are very good at providing single target damage. They’re able to attack ground and air troops which is very helpful. You’ll have the same number of archer towers that you did at Town Hall 5. You should place the three archer towers on the perimeter of your base so that they can protect the buildings outside of the walls as well as the cannon and mortars.

Cannon – The cannon is a great unit for picking off individual troops. It can only target ground troops which means it’s vulnerable to air units. You’ll have the same number of cannons that you did at the previous town hall. Place the three cannons on the perimeter of your base. They will be very helpful in providing protection to buildings outside of your walls.

Mortar – The mortar is a very important defensive unit. It does splash damage and becomes increasingly more powerful as units get clumped up trying to reach the center of your base. You’ll gain an extra mortar at this level for a total of two. Place the two mortars near the center of your base. They’ll be next to your Town Hall and on opposite sides of each other.

Air Defense – The air defense does tremendous damage to air units. It’s a great building for providing defense to areas of your base that are weak to air attacks. You won’t gain an extra building at this town hall level so you’ll still only have one air defense. Place the air defense near the center of your base to provide additional air coverage across your entire base.
