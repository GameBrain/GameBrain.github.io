Trap Overview

The Spring Trap will instantly remove enemy troops from battle when it’s activated. The trap will cause the attacking troops to be launched off of the screen.

We suggest that you don’t place Spring Traps too closely to bomb traps such as the Bomb and Giant Bomb. An enemy unit could accidentally trigger both the Spring Trap and the bomb to go off. Since the Spring Trap instantly removes the unit from battle, it’s possible that the bomb would explode without doing damage to any other units.

Trap Information

Damage type: Single target

Favorite target: Any

Targets: Ground
