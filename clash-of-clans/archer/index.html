Troop Overview

The archer is a powerful ranged damage dealer in Clash of Clans. The troop can do a lot of damage from afar but doesn’t have as much hitpoints as other troops. The archer should be paired with a melee unit so that the troop can distract towers from targeting the archer.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground and air

Housing space: 1

Training time: 25 seconds

Movement speed: 24

Troop Models

The graphic models for each level of the archer are shown below. The archer’s bow, hair, quiver and clothes are the big changes at each level.

Level 1 – 2: At level one and two the archer has a brown bow, green clothes, pink tips on the arrows and pink hair.

Level 3 – 4: The only difference when the unit is upgraded to level three is that the model’s hair will change to purple.

Level 5: At level five the model will change her hair back to the original pink color and will start to use a green hood which covers most of her hair.

Level 6: The archer will lose her hood at level six and her hair will change to a dark purple. She’ll also start to use a golden headband at this level and the tips of the arrows will switch from pink to dark purple.

Level 7: The center of the archer’s headband changes at this level. It changes from pure gold to gold with a purple circle in it.
