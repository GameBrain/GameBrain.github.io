Troop Overview

The Healer is an air unit that heals friendly targets by sending beams of light to them. The beams of light heal all targets within a small radius. This unit can’t attack so it needs the help of other troops to distract towers and prevent the towers from targeting the Healer.

Air Defense towers can be very dangerous to the healer. Make sure your other troops are able to destroy them quickly or else the healer will be very vulnerable.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 14

Training time: 15 minutes

Movement speed: 16
