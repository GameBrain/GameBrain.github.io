Troop Overview

The Hog Rider is a melee unit that rides a pet and targets defensive buildings. The unit can jump over walls and destroy defensive buildings very quickly.

Other troops cannot jump over walls so it can be difficult at times for them to stay with your Hog Riders during a raid. You can assist your other troops by using a Jump Spell. A common strategy is to use Hog Riders as your entire raid composition. Healing Spells are a good choice if you use an entire Hog Rider army.
