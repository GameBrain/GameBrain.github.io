Troop Overview

The Golem is a very tough melee tanking unit. Upon death, the unit will explode and spawn several miniature versions of itself called Golemites.

The Golem is usually used in end game army compositions. It works very nicely with strong ranged damage dealers such as the Wizard and the Witch.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Defenses

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 30

Training time: 45 minutes

Movement speed: 12
