Overview

Spells are what you use to help your troops in battle. If your army is weak in one area you use a spell to improve it. Spells are created in the Spell Factory building.

Spells can be upgraded to make them stronger just like buildings and troops. All spell upgrades are researched in the Laboratory building.

Types of Spells

Lightning – Strike a small area of the battlefield and deals damage to enemy units.

Healing – Heals friendly targets within a large radius.

Rage – Increases friendly troop’s damage and speed.

Jump – Gives friendly troops the ability to jump over walls.

Freeze – Disables enemy towers for a short amount of time.

Santa’s Surprise – A more powerful version of the Lightning Spell.
