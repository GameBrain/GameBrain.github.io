Building Overview

The Builder’s Hut is a resource building that let’s you hire an additional builder for your village. Builders can remove obstacles from your base and they’re also used to upgrade your buildings.

Builders are usually the bottleneck when it comes to upgrading your village so we recommend getting all five Builder’s Huts as soon as you can.
