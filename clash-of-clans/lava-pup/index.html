Troop Overview

The Lava Pups are spawned when the Lava Hound dies on the battlefield. The Lava Pups don’t have as much HP as the hound but they will still be helpful in distracting towers.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground and air

Housing space: -

Training time: -

Movement speed: 32
