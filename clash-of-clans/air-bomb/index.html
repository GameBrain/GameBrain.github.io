Trap Overview

The Air Bomb is similar to the Bomb but instead of targeting ground units the Air Bomb targets air units. The Air Bomb has a large splash radius so make sure to place the trap in an area where air troops are likely to get grouped up.

Trap Information

Trigger radius: 5 tiles

Damage type: Area splash

Favorite target: Any

Targets: Air

Trap Models

The graphic models for each level of the trap are shown below. The Air Bomb’s model consists of two balloons attached to a bomb. The bomb is hidden in a trap door which will open when air units are close.
