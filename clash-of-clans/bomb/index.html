Trap Overview

The Bomb is a trap which deals splash damage to ground troops when it’s triggered. Place the trap in an area where it’s likely to hit more than one unit to take advantage of the splash damage.

Trap Information

Trigger radius: 1 tile

Damage type: Area splash

Favorite target: Any

Targets: Ground
