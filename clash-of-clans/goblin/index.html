Troop Overview

The goblin is a speedy unit that loves nothing more than stealing loot from your enemy. Goblins have a special ability which lets them do twice as much damage when they’re attacking resource buildings. They can also be used to trigger traps and bombs because they can run way in enough time and not take any damage.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Resources (Damage x2)

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 1

Training time: 30 seconds

Movement speed: 32

Troop Models

The graphic models for each level of the goblin are shown below. The goblin’s bag, hair and clothes are the things that change in each model.

Level 1 – 2: At the first and second level the goblin has a brown bag, no hair and brown shorts.

Level 3 – 4: The bag that the goblin is carrying will change to a different color at level three and four. The color will change from a solid dark brown to a striped pattern with dark and light brown colors.

Level 5: At level five the goblin decides to change his hair. He goes from being bald to growing a dark red mohawk. The bag that he’s carrying will also change to a light brown color.

Level 6: The goblin will undergo a change to his clothing at level six. The unit will cover up his suspenders by wearing a brown shirt.
