Troop Overview

The Archer Queen is a ranged hero unit in Clash of Clans. The Queen is similar to the Archer and it has a special hero ability that can be activated during a raid. The ability is called Royal Cloak and it will increase the Archer Queen’s damage, recover health and vanish her from sight for a small period of time. The Royal Cloak ability will also summon Archers to help the Queen.

The Archer Queen is best paired with a tanking troop like the Giant and Golem. The Queen doesn’t have as much HP as other units and works best by attacking from afar. If a tower happens to target the Archer Queen you can use her special ability to vanish from sight and recover HP.
