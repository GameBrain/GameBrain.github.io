Building Overview

The Barracks is used to build troops that cost elixir. Once the troop is created they will walk to the army camp where they will stay until you use them in an attack.

Upgrading the Barracks will unlock new troops for you to use. While the building is being upgraded you won’t be able to use it to create troops. You can try upgrading all of your Barracks at once to minimize the impact to your raid schedule

You can queue Troops before you start searching for enemy players to attack. Your Barracks will create Troops from the time you’ve started your search to the time you’ve completed the attack. If you have enough housing space the troops will be added to your army camp when you return to your village after a battle. We advise you to queue up troops before an attack because it can significantly reduce the amount of downtime between fights.

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the Barracks are shown below. The building is a wooden hut and most models add an addition to the building which symbolizes the newly unlocked unit.

The level two version of the building has a bulls eye target because that’s when Archers are unlocked. Level five has mini bombs outside of the entrance because that’s when Wall Breakers are unlocked.
