Building Overview

The Laboratory building is used to upgrade your troops and spells. It’s best to focus on upgrading the troops and spells that you use frequently so that you can continue to farm and get resources faster.

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the Laboratory are shown below. The building starts off as a normal looking hut and quickly changes into a building that looks like it’s from a sci-fi movie. It gets liquid chambers and metal coils added to it as it’s upgraded.
