Spell Overview

The Freeze Spell is used to help your troops in battle by disabling enemy towers for a period of time. A blue frost ring will appear on the ground when the Freeze Spell is used. Enemy towers within the ring will not be able to function for a small amount of time.

Take full advantage of the spell by using it on enemy towers that will do the most damage to your army. The Inferno Tower and the X-Bow are usually good targets to freeze because they’ll be able to do a lot of damage to your army regardless of its composition.

Spell Information

Effect type: Area splash

Target type: Ground

Time to create: 45 minutes
