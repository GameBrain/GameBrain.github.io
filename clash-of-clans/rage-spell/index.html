Spell Overview

The Rage Spell is used to help your troops in battle by increasing their damage and their speed. A large purple ring will appear on the ground when the Rage Spell is used. Troops within the ring will receive the damage and speed boost.

The Rage Spell is helpful to all troops but we’ve found that it benefits slower troops the most. Troops like the Dragon and the Golem are very slow and the spell lets them get to their targets much faster.

Take full advantage of the spell by dropping it slightly ahead of the troops and in the direction that they are moving. This will maximize the amount of time that the troops are within the ring and receive the damage and speed increase.

Spell Information

Effect type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Time to create: 45 minutes
