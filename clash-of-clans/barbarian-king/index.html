Troop Overview

The Barbarian King is a melee hero unit in Clash of Clans. It’s similar to the Barbarian and it has a special hero ability that can be used during raids. Iron Fist is the Barbarian King’s special ability. The ability increases the King’s damage, speed and also heals him. The ability will also summon Barbarians and boost the attack power and attack speed of nearby Barbarian troops.

The Barbarian King can be used as a tank since it has a lot of hitpoints and can recover health when it uses its special ability. You can still use other troops to help distract towers but you have the option to reduce the number of tanking troops since the Barbarian King doesn’t take up any housing space.

We recommend pairing the Barbarian King with Barbarians when you can since the Iron Fist ability will benefit nearby Barbarian troops.
