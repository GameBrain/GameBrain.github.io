Troop Overview

The Witch is a mighty summoner who raises Skeletons from the dead to attack her enemy. The Witch is a fragile unit that needs to be protected by a tanking unit such as the Golem.

Like other troops, the longer the Witch is on the battlefield the more damage she is able to do. Since she raises Skeletons from the dead she can also help keep your army alive the longer she’s on the battlefield. Towers can focus on the Skeletons rather than your other troops. Make sure that the Witch is well protected when you use her during a raid.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Housing space: 12

Training time: 20 minutes

Movement speed: 12
