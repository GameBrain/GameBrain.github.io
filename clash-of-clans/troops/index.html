Overview

Troops are what you use to attack other players in Clash of Clans. They let you steal resources and trophies from your opponents by raiding their village. Raids are the primary way to get resources to upgrade your base.

Troops can be grouped into a few categories. One category for elixir troops, another for dark elixir troops and one for hero units. Elixir units are created in the Barracks and dark elixir units are created in the Dark Barracks. Hero units are immortal and are only created once.

Units can be upgraded to make them stronger just like buildings. All troop upgrades are researched in the laboratory building.

Elixir Troops

Barbarian – Single target melee damage dealer.

Archer – Single target ranged damage dealer.

Giant – Melee tanking unit that prefers to attack defensive units.

Goblin – Melee unit that loves to target resource buildings.

Wall Breaker – Melee unit that is perfect for breaking through walls.

Balloon – Tough air unit that targets defensive buildings.

Wizard – Extremely powerful ranged damage dealer.

Healer – Air unit that heals friendly targets.

Dragon – Very strong air unit that does splash damage.

P.E.K.K.A – Very tough melee unit.

Dark Elixir Troops

Minion – Quick and versatile flying unit.

Hog Rider – Melee unit that rides a pet and targets defensive buildings.

Valkyrie – Fierce melee warrior that does splash damage to ground units

Golem – Very tough melee tanking unit that spawns Golemites upon death.

Witch – Mighty summoner who raises Skeletons from the dead to attack her enemy.

Hero Troops

Barbarian King – Powerful melee unit that can increase damage of nearby Barbarians.

Archer Queen – Lethal ranged damage dealer that can vanish from battle.
