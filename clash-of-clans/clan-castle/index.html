Building Overview

The Clan Castle is your primary building for interacting with clans. It lets you join an existing clan or create your own. The building will also store your loot bonus from clan wars.

The Clan Castle also lets you request troops from your clan mates. The troops can be used to defend your village during an attack or they can be used to assist you in a raid. Remember to return the favor when a clan mate asks for troops.

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the Clan Castle are shown below. The building starts off as a ruin and will need to be repaired before you’re able to use it. The building is repaired with stone blocks at level 1 and the stone is used in most of the lower level models. The higher level models replace the stone with steel.
