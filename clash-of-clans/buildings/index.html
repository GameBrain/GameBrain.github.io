Overview

Buildings are what you use to create your base in Clash of Clans. There are many different buildings and most of them can be categorized as offensive or defensive buildings. Learn more about specific buildings by following the links below.

Army Buildings

The army buildings are used to create troops that you can use to attack other players in the game. The Laboratory and the Spell Factory are used to help improve your army and make it more powerful.

Army Camp – Used to house your troops.

Barracks – Creates troops that cost elixir.

Dark Barracks – Creates troops that cost dark elixir.

Laboratory – Used to research powerful upgrades for your troops and spells.

Spell Factory – Creates spells that can enhance your army in battle.

Defensive Buildings

Defensive buildings are what you will use to protect your village from other players. Other players can attack your village when you’re offline so use the buildings below to create a strong defense and protect your resources and trophies.

Archer Tower – Single target damage tower that can attack both air and ground troops.

Cannon – Single target damage tower that can only attack ground troops.

Mortar – Attacks slowly but deals massive splash damage to ground troops.

Wizard Tower – It attacks fast, targets ground and air and deals splash damage.

Air Defense – Deals tremendous damage to air units very quickly.

X-Bow – Versatile tower that can be toggled to attack any unit in the game.

Inferno Tower – Deal enormous damage to one unit or attack multiple units.

Wall – Main protection for your village.

Resource Buildings

Resources buildings are used to accumulate gold, elixir and dark elixir. The resources are used to upgrade buildings, troops and more.

Gold Mine – Collector that produces gold every hour.

Elixir Collector – Collector that creates elixir every hour.

Dark Elixir Drill – Collector that makes dark elixir every hour.

Gold Storage – Storage unit for gold resources.

Elixir Storage – Storage depot for elixir resources.

Dark Elixir Storage – Storage unit for dark elixir resources.

Builder’s Hut – Let’s you hire additional workers for your village.

Traps

Traps are used to help defend your base against an attack in Clash of Clans. Traps are hidden to an attacking player which gives you an advantage when placing them in your base.

Not all traps can be triggered by every unit. Some will only be triggered by air units while others can only be triggered by ground units. Make sure you place the traps in areas of your base where you need extra defense.

Bomb – Deals splash damage to ground troops.

Spring Trap – Instantly removes several troops from battle.

Giant Bomb – More powerful version of the Bomb.

Air Bomb – Deals splash damage to all air units.

Seeking Air Mine – Follows target and deals massive damage to a single target.

Skeleton Trap – Releases skeletons to attack your enemy.

Other Buildings

Town Hall – The heart of your village. Upgrading it unlocks new buildings.

Clan Castle – The primary building for interacting with clans.
