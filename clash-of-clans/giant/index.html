Troop Overview

The Giant is a slow tanking unit used to distract towers. Because of the Giant’s size and ability to take a lot of damage, it is great to pair with troops that do a lot of damage but have lower HP. The archer is a great unit to pair with the giant.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Defenses

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 5

Training time: 2 minutes

Movement speed: 12
