Troop Overview

The Skeleton is summoned from the dead to fight in battle by the Witch. The Witch is able to summon more Skeletons the higher level she is.

Skeletons are an amazing troop because they can help the rest of your army stay alive longer during a raid. Skeletons can act as a tank to distract towers and allow your other units to not take as much damage.
