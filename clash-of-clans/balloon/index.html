Troop Overview

The Balloon is a very tough air unit that targets defensive buildings. It’s manned by several Wall Breakers who drop bombs on their target. The Balloon works very well with other air troops that are able to attack all types of units such as the Minion and the Dragon.

When the Balloon dies it also does splash damage to all units directly below it. This can be used to kill enemy troops from an opponent’s clan castle. Lure the troops to an area on the map and make sure they are close together. Send one Balloon directly on top of the troops and when it dies it will deal splash damage to all enemy troops.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Defenses

Damage type: Area splash
