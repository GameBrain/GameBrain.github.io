Building Overview

The Town Hall is the heart of your village and it determines which buildings and troops you can use. When you upgrade your Town Hall you’ll have access to more powerful buildings, troops and spells. We recommend that you finish most of the available upgrades (buildings, walls, ect) at each Town Hall level before you decide to upgrade.

It becomes increasingly harder to get resources as you upgrade the Town Hall because of a loot penalty system that Clash of Clans uses. You’ll get less available loot for attacking villages that are lower than yours. The system discourages higher level players from attacking lower level players.

You’ll automatically lose a raid if the attacking player can destroy your Town Hall. You can use this to your benefit by using a farming base. Farming bases prioritize protecting resources rather than trophies. Most farming bases will place the town hall in an area of the map where it can easily be destroyed. You give up your town and “trade” trophies for a shield which means you can’t be attacked for at least 12 hours.
