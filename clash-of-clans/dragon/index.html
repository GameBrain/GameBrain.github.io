Troop Overview

The Dragon is a very strong air unit. It moves slowly but deals tremendous damage when it attacks. The Dragon is one of the few units that can be used (successfully) as an entire army composition.

The only downfall to the unit is its speed. Air Defense towers can do significant damage to the unit before it’s able to reach the tower. Make sure you’re able to clear the Air Defense towers quickly. You can use a Rage Spell to help your troops reach the tower faster or you can pair the Dragon with other units who have a faster movement speed.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Housing space: 20

Training time: 30 minutes

Movement speed: 16
