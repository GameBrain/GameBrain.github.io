Spell Overview

The Lightning Spell will strike a small area on the map multiple times and doing damage to the area with each strike. The spell can be used to destroy enemy units and buildings.

The Lightning Spell can be used to destroy troops from an enemy’s Clan Castle. Make sure that you lure the troops to one area of the map and make sure that they are close together before dropping the spell.

You can use the Lightning Spell to “snipe” Dark Elixir from your opponent. Make sure to zoom in as far as you can before dropping all of the spells on the storage that way they’re more accurate. This strategy is frowned upon by many players because you’re able to bypass their entire defense to steal the resource.

Spell Information

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Time to create: 30 minutes
