Building Overview

The mortar is an amazing tower that attacks slowly and deals massive splash damage. The mortar cannot attack air units so it will need to be placed by a tower that can. The mortar also has a small range around the tower where it’s unable to attack troops. Make sure that it’s difficult for enemy troops to get into this area or else the mortar will be vulnerable and unable to attack.

Building Information

Range: 4 – 11 tiles

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Favorite target: Any
