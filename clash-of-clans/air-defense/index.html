Building Overview

The air defense tower does tremendous damage to air units. It’s the best anti air tower in the game and it’s a great building for providing defense to areas of your base that are weak to air attacks. It’s a great tower to place near your mortars since they can only attack ground troops.

Building Information

Range: 10 tiles

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Air

Favorite target: Any

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the air defense tower are shown below. Levels one through three use a single wooden barrel to hold rockets and each level has an additional rocket in the barrel. Starting at level four, the rockets change colors. The rockets will now be yellow, red and black instead of just red and black. Level four and five have two wooden barrels each holding several rockets in them. Level six adds an additional barrel to the tower for a total of three. Level seven and eight use one giant barrel to hold lots of rockets in them. Level eight uses gold and steel for the base of the tower.
