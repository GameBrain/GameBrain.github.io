Troop Overview

The P.E.K.K.A is a very tough melee unit. The P.E.K.K.A attacks slowly but hits very hard. The unit is expensive to produce and because of that it’s usually used in end game army compositions. The unit works very well with strong ranged damage dealers like the Wizard.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Any

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 25

Training time: 45 minutes

Movement speed: 16
