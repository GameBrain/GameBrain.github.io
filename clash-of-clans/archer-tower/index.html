Building Overview

Archer towers are a versatile defensive building in Clash of Clans. They’re able to provide great single target damage and attack both air and ground troops. The tower’s quick attack speed also helps to pick off troops faster than other towers.

Archer Towers are great way to provide extra air defense to your base. We suggest placing them near Cannons and Mortars.

Building Information

Range: 10 tiles

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground and air

Favorite target: Any
