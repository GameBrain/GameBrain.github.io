Troop Overview

The Minion is a very quick air unit. It has a very fast attack speed, quick build time and is the least expensive Dark Elixir unit in the game. These traits make the unit a versatile troop which can be used in several successful army compositions.

The Minion cannot be targeted by a Seeking Air Mine but it can be targeted by a Air Bomb. Make sure that the units don’t get clumped up or else a single Air Bomb will destroy a lot them. This can be difficult to do because of how quick the unit is. Try sending a few Minions ahead of the rest of your troops to trigger bombs. It’s much better to lose a few troop rather than your entire army.

Troop Information
