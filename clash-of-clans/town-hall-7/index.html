This Clash of Clans Town Hall 7 guide will show you how to use different base designs to maximize your loot and show you how to defend against attacks and protect your trophies.

Overview

Town hall 7 is all about Dark Elixir. Dark Elixir is an additional resource that can be used to build powerful troops and hero units.

The Dark Elixir drill (and storage) is most important building that youâ€™ll unlock at this level. The drill behaves the same way as the gold mine and elixir collector and will produce dark elixir for you. We suggest that you upgrade the drill as soon as you can because it will provide you with a steady income of Dark Elixir.

You’ll also unlock the Dark Elixir Storage building. Similar to other storage buildings, this building lets you store Dark Elixir.

As we mentioned above, dark elixir can be used to add powerful units to your army. The Dark Elixir Barracks is a new building at this level that will let you train new army troops at the cost of Dark Elixir. BAM (Barbarian, Archer, Minion) is a great way to start experimenting with using Dark Elixir troops. BAM is the fastest army to build and is great for raiding!

Another very important addition to Town Hall 7 is your first hero unit. The Barbarian King is a mighty warrior that can be used for assisting your troops in an attack or for defending your base when you’re away. We recommend that you save up your dark elixir and get the Barbarian King as soon as you can.

The last unit that you’ll unlock is the Seeking Air Mine. The Seeking Air mine is a trap that will follow a single air target and will explode dealing massive damage to it. This is ideal for taking down an air targets with a lot of health.
