Building Overview

The Gold Mine is a resource collector building. It will produce gold every hour that can be used to upgrade defensive buildings, walls, rearm traps and more. The gold that’s produced will be stored in a Gold Storage building.

You can use gems on the Gold Mine to speed it up and produce twice as much gold per hour.
