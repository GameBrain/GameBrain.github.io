Troop Overview

If you’re having a tough time getting through walls, the wall breaker is the perfect troop for you! This unit targets enemy walls and tries to blow them up. Once the walls are blown up it will be easier for your troops to get through and attack the enemy base.

Troop Information

Favorite target: Walls (Damage x40)

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground

Housing space: 2

Training time: 2 minutes

Movement speed: 24
