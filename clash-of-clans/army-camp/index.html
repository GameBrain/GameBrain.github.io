Building Overview

The Army Camp is used to house your troops. Increasing the camp’s level will also increase the housing space which lets you use more troops in a raid. We suggest that you upgrade the Army Camp as soon as possible because it will make your attacks much stronger.

The Army Camp is a large building and it takes up a lot of space on the map. The Army Camp’s size can be used to your advantage when defending attacks. Place the camp in an area of your defense that is weak to ground units. The Army Camp will help your defense by keeping the attacking units further away from your base and giving your towers more time to attack the enemy troops.
