General Information

This Clash of Clans Town Hall 9 guide will show you how to use different base designs to maximize your loot and show you how to defend against attacks and protect your trophies.

Our guides are created and maintained using the most recent data available from the game. We’ve done extensive play-testing to help us give you the most effective strategy for achieving your goals. We value feedback from our visitors and if you have any suggestions for this guide please feel free to contact us.

Overview

The only new building that will be unlocked at Town Hall 9 is the X-Bow. The X-Bow is a very powerful single target defensive building. It can be toggled between attacking ground troops or ground and air troops. If it’s set to attack ground units it will get a boost in the range in which it can attack troops. If it’s set to ground and air troops the X-Bow will have a medium range. Whether or not you have the X-Bow set to ground troops or ground and air depends on the trophy range that you’re in. If you’re in a farming trophy range (low trophies) you’ll most likely be better off having the X-Bow be set to attack ground troops because most of the common army compositions are ground units. As you work your way up in trophies you may need to adjust the X-Bow setting as you face different army compositions. The strongest army composition right now consists of all air units. We recommend setting the X-Bow to air and ground units if you start getting attacked consistently by Balloons, Minions and Lava Hounds.
