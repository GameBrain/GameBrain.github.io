Building Overview

Cannons are your standard defensive building in Clash of Clans and they’re the first tower that you’ll unlock in the game. They target single units and are ideal for picking off ground troops. Cannons can only target ground troops so you’ll need to make sure they’ve got some form of air defense nearby to protect it from air units.

Building Information

Range: 9 tiles

Damage type: Single target

Target type: Ground

Favorite target: Any
