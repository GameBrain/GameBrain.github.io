General Information

On this page of our guide, we will explain what the best upgrades are for Town Hall 6 in Clash of Clans and how you’ll benefit from each of them.

The upgrade order is important because it will allow you to raid more efficiently and protect your hard earned resources from attacking players.

TH6 Upgrade Priority

The upgrade priority for Town Hall 6 is:

  1. Laboratory
  2. Army Camps
  3. Spell Factory
  4. Gold Mines & Elixir Collectors
  5. Gold Storage & Elixir Storage
  6. Wizard Towers
  7. Air Defenses
  8. Mortars
  9. Archer Towers
  10. Cannons
  11. Air Sweeper
  12. Traps
  13. Walls

TH6 Upgrade Summary

Laboratory

We believe that the the Laboratory building should be the first thing to upgrade at Town Hall 6. The reason behind that is because you’ll unlock several key upgrades for your troops that benefit you in wars, farming and trophy pushing. The upgrades are mentioned below in the Laboratory section of this page.

Army Camp

This increase the size of the army that you can use during a raid. This is really important because having more troops to use will improve your chances of winning an attack.

Spell Factory

Unlocks a new spell which can be used to heal your troops during battle. The upgrade will also allow you to queue and bring an additional spell to combat.

Gold Mines & Elixir Collectors

These upgrades will provide you with more Gold and Elixir per hour than the previous level. They’re a great way to generate resources when you’re unable to raid.

Gold Storage & Elixir Storage

Allows you to store more Gold and Elixir than the previous level. You’ll need this as building upgrades start to get more costly.

Wizard Towers

Very good defensive tower that deals splash damage to ground and air troops.

Air Defense

Deals tremendous damage to air units. Without strong anti air towers your base doesn’t stand a chance at stopping mass air units.

Mortars

Slow but powerful defensive unit that deals splash damage to ground troops. This is an important tower for dealing with mass ground troops.

Archer Towers

Strong single target tower that deals damage to all troops. We recommend going for splash damage before towers that deal single target damage.

Cannons

Powerful tower that does single target damage to ground troops.

Air Sweeper

Makes it harder for air troops to attack your base. Mass air units can be difficult to deal with so this tower helps to prevent the troops from reaching critical buildings.

Traps

These towers can quickly put the battle in your favor if you catch the attacker off guard. Most need to be rearmed after they’ve been used in battle so we prefer to upgrade the other buildings first.

Walls

You can upgrade your walls sooner in the priority list if you have extra resources to spend. We recommend that you max all of your walls before moving on to the next Town Hall level.

TH6 Laboratory Upgrade Priority

  1. Wall Breaker
  2. Giant
  3. Healing Spell
  4. Wizard
  5. Balloon

TH6 Laboratory Upgrade Summary

Wall Breaker

There’s a few reasons why the Wall Breaker should be your first Laboratory upgrade. The first reason is that by upgrading the unit to the max level they will now be able to break through your opponent’s walls with one less unit. This frees up space in your army and allows you to bring other units that can be used for tanking or dealing damage.

The second reason why this unit should be upgraded first is because it can be used in any army composition. This upgrade directly benefits farming as well as wars and trophy pushing.

Giant

The Giant is your primary tanking unit at this level. It’s a core unit for your war and trophy army. It’s important to upgrade your tanking units first otherwise your damage dealing troops won’t stay alive very long on the battlefield. The Giant can also be used in farming which is an added bonus.

Healing Spell

This is the spell of choice for Town Hall 6. It can be used in any army composition and it works very well with Giants. You should upgrade the spell after your important troop upgrades because a strong spell will not be able to make up for weak units.

Wizard

The Wizard is a powerful damage dealing troop but it comes at a cost. It’s an expensive unit to make at Town Hall 6 and will primarily be used for wars and trophy pushing. Since there’s limited use for the unit in farming we recommend upgrading it later than the other choices.

Balloon

We saved the Balloon upgrade for last. Although it’s a fun unit to use there are more viable army compositions for farming, wars and trophy pushing. We feel that the Balloon becomes more viable at a higher level Town Hall.
