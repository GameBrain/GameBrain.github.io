Building Overview

The wizard tower is an amazing defensive unit. It attacks very fast, can target ground and air troops, and it deals splash damage. The wizard tower doesn’t hit as hard as some of the other towers so you’ll need to take advantage of choke points in your base to use the tower to its full potential.

Building Information

Range: 7 tiles

Damage type: Area splash

Target type: Ground and air

Favorite target: Any

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the wizard tower are shown below. The lower level towers look a lot like a pile of rocks. As the tower starts to level up, purple crystals start to show through the rocks. The crystals will continue to be more visible on the tower up until level 6 where it will undergo a major change. The wizard tower will no longer have stones in it and will consist entirely of purple crystals. The crystal color starts off as a light purple at level 6 and moves toward a deep purple at level 8. The platform where the wizard stands will also become a little larger on level 8.
