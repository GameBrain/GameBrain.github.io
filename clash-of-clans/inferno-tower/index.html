Building Overview

The Inferno Tower is a very strong defensive building that can be toggled to deal massive single target damage or attack multiple units at once. When the tower is set to single targets it will increase the DPS done to the unit the longer the target is alive. When the tower is set to attack multiple units it will lose the ability to increase DPS the longer it stays on a target. The Inferno Tower is powered by Dark Elixir and uses it for ammo. The tower will need to be refilled with Dark Elixir when it runs out of ammo.

Building Information

Range: 9 tiles

Damage type: Single target / Multiple targets

Target type: Ground and Air

Favorite target: Any

Building Models

The graphic models for each level of the Inferno Tower are shown below. The tower looks fairly similar for each level. The tower has dark steel, gold and fire for the design at each level. Level one is mostly dark steel and has a fiery circle on top of the tower. Level two introduces more gold into the design and it has more gold in the shaft and at the top of it. Level three has more steel at the top of the tower and the fiery effect becomes much more noticeable at this level.
