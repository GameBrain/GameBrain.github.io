Introduction

Welcome to the Heroes of the Storm guide for Valla. This guide is intended for maximizing the hero’s effectiveness in the game by using an optimal talent build and playstyle.

Valla is an excellent ranged damage dealer in Heroes of the Storm. She’s capable of sustaining tremendous single-target damage to any hero within her attack range.

Valla players are expected to bring lots of damage to a teamfight while remaining safely among your teammates. Positioning is very important because Valla does not have a lot of health and is an easy target for the enemy team to kill.

Talent Build

This is the recommended talent build for Valla. This Valla build is based on extensive play testing to determine the best talent choices for each level. We believe this is the optimal way to play the hero but your play style and team composition may alter the suggested build.

Level 1

Composite Arrows – This is the preferred level 1 talent because it increases the range of multishot and lets you do damage from a safer distance.

Rancor – Causes Hatred stacks to increase Valla’s attack speed. This is a good choice if you don’t like Composite Arrows.

Level 4

Puncturing Arrow – This choice increases the range of Hungering Arrow and allows it to strike an additional target.

Level 7

Repeating Arrow – Using Vault refreshes Hungering Arrow’s cooldown. This lets you apply additional pressure when needed.

Level 10

Strafe – Attacks all enemies in a large radius. Best used when your team does not have a lot of players who can stun or slow targets.

Rain of Vengeance – Launches two shock waves in a given area. This is best used when your teammates have stunning abilities or have abilities to slow a target.

Level 13

Frost Shot – Causes Multishot to reduce the movement speed of all targets. Works very well for peeling heroes off of teammates and for preventing an enemy from running away.

Level 16

Blood for Blood – Active ability to steal health from a target and slow its movement speed. Provides strong burst damage and self-healing.

Level 20

Bolt of the Storm – Allows you to instantly teleport to a nearby location. This talent is very helpful when you’re caught in a bad position.

Strengths

  • Very high damage
  • Great mobility
  • Fits in any team composition

Weaknesses

  • Low health
  • Requires good positioning
  • Minimal early game defensive cooldowns
