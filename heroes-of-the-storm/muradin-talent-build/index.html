Overview

On this page of the guide, we will show you the recommended talent build for Muradin in Heroes of the Storm. The recommendations are based on extensive play testing to determine the best talent choices for each level. We’ve provided a reason for choosing each talent below.

Talent Build

Below is the recommended Muradin build that outlines which talents to take at each level. We believe this is the optimal way to play the hero but your play style and team composition may alter the suggested build.

Level 1

Infused Hammer – This talent will allow your Storm Bolt to refund mana whenever it hits a target. As a melee hero it can be difficult to trade damage with other heroes, especially when they’re ranged. This choice will allow you to trade damage more reliably by being able to use Storm Bolt very often. It’s also helpful because it allows you stay in your lane much longer without having to leave to regenerate your mana from hearthing or drinking from a pool.

Level 4

Crowd Control – This choice will reduce the cooldown on Thunder Clap for each target hit with it. The talent is very helpful with clearing large enemy minion waves quickly. It will also help to boost your overall DPS in a teamfight. Muradin is usually in the center of teamfights so Thunder Clap will hit multiple targets thus reducing the cooldown and giving you more overall damage.

Level 7

Piercing Bolt – This talent lets your Storm Bolt pierce through the first target and hit 1 additional target. This allows you to stun more than one hero during a teamfight which can be extremely helpful. You’ll also be refunded twice as much mana because of taking the Infused Hammer talent.

Level 10

Avatar – Provides a large boost to Muradin’s HP and allows auto attacks to stun their target. The increase to HP is very helpful because you’ll stay alive longer and help your team to control the fight.

Level 13

Thunder Strike – Makes your Thunderclap ability deal triple damage if it only hits one target. This is great for 1v1 scenarios or dealing additional damage to heroes that are not near their teammates.

Level 16

Stoneform – This talent lets you activate a strong heal over time spell that heals for a percentage of your health. It scales very well with the Avatar talent. Make sure to activate Avatar before using Stoneform.

Level 20

Unstoppable Force – This talent makes the Avatar ability last longer and it reduces the duration of all crowd control against you. This talent significantly increases your ability to stay alive in teamfights and gives you more opportunity to control the engagements and put the battle in your team’s favor.
