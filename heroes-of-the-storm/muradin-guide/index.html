Introduction

Welcome to the Heroes of the Storm guide for Muradin. This guide is intended for maximizing the hero’s effectiveness in the game by using an optimal talent build and playstyle.

Muradin is one of the most durable warriors in Heroes of the Storm. He’s capable of controlling engagements and dictating the pace of team fights through his use of abilities that can stun and slow enemy heroes.

Muradin players are expected to protect teammates by drawing the attention of the enemy to themselves. Players are also expected to use Muradin’s crowd control abilities to peel off enemy heroes that are focusing on your teammates.

Strengths

  • Very hard to kill
  • Passive HP regen
  • Has several crowd control abilities

Muradin’s very difficult to kill because of his large health pool, his heroic trait and his crowd control abilities. Second Wind lets him regenerate HP faster when he’s out of combat and can be used to stay on the battle field without having to hearth and heal himself. Players can use his Dwarf Toss ability to leap out of dangerous positions or use a combination of Storm Bolt and Thunderclap to stun and slow an enemy while running away to safety.

Weaknesses

  • Lacks burst damage
  • Storm Bolt can be difficult to aim
  • Not very good at clearing mercenary camps

Muradin doesn’t have a lot of burst damage which means you’ll need to coordinate attacks with your teammates to get a kill on an enemy hero. Storm Bolt can be crucial to the coordinated attack and very often can mean the difference between getting or not getting the kill. You’ll also depend on your teammates to clear mercenary camps because without them it can be very difficult.
